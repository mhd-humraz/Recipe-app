Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

25 mins 4 servings Medium

Ingredients

Instructions

1

Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook pasta according to package instructions.

2

In a pan, sauté garlic in butter until fragrant (1-2 mins).

3

Add heavy cream and simmer for 3 mins. Stir in parmesan until melted.
